# AdoptionLens

**Status: Preview** · *The core "are people actually using it?" lens — usage, reach, and activation.*

## What it measures

- Who is **active** on Copilot, and how much of the enabled population that is.
- How **widely** Copilot has spread across teams, apps, and surfaces.
- How engagement **trends** over time (activation, retention, reach).

## Unlocking data source(s)

| Source | Role | Required? |
|---|---|---|
| Audit log | Copilot usage events | **Core** |

## Key pages / views

- **Activation**
- **Adoption & Reach**
- **Heatmap Trend**

## Setup

AdoptionLens runs entirely from the core audit log — no optional sources required. See [PREREQUISITES.md](../ValueLens/docs/PREREQUISITES.md) and pick a deployment path: [Fabric](../ValueLens/deployment/1.%20Fabric/README.md) or [SharePoint (PAX)](../ValueLens/deployment/2.%20SharePoint/README.md).
