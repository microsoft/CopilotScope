ValueLens

Value & ROI

One Power BI template for every Copilot and agent adoption signal. ValueLens turns Purview audit logs, licensing data, and org structure into a defensible ROI narrative — hours saved, assisted value, adoption, maturity, and governance across your whole Copilot estate, aligned to Microsoft's Frontier Firm framework.

15report pages
3deployment paths
Publishedtime-baseline research

What it shows

Every Copilot interaction is classified into an AI Task, mapped to a research-sourced time baseline, and turned into hours saved and assisted value.

  • Quantified value

    Hours saved and assisted dollar value across your whole Copilot estate.

  • Frontier Firm journey

    Your position on the path from human + Copilot, to human + agent, to agent-run workflows.

  • Value by function

    Sales, HR, IT, Legal, Finance, Marketing, and Customer Service, with task-level attribution.

  • User maturity

    Every user placed on the curve from Beginner through Proficient.

  • Business case

    Projected annual value, ROI multiple, and license investment net.

How the value model works

  1. Interaction
  2. AI Task
  3. Time baseline
  4. Hours saved
  5. × Rate = Assisted value

Grounded in published research from Microsoft Research, MIT, NBER, BCG/Harvard, McKinsey, Forrester, IDC and others.
