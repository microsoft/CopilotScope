# MaturityLens

**Status: Preview** · *The adoption proficiency curve — Initial to Efficient — with a Maturity Meter.*

## What it measures

- Where the population sits on the **maturity curve** (Initial to Efficient).
- How maturity is **progressing** over time.
- Which cohorts are advancing, and which are stuck at early stages.

## Unlocking data source(s)

| Source | Role | Required? |
|---|---|---|
| Audit log (`User_Stage` / `_Maturity`) | Maturity-stage classification | **Core** |

## Key pages / views

- **Usage Maturity**
